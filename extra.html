  • Director of Finance

    DUET Computer Society, DUET

    DUET Computer Society colloquially referred to as the DUETCS is a one of the renowned society in DUET which was established in 2010. It comprises and represents all undergraduates pursuing a degree in Department of CSE. The purpose of the DUETCS is to provide an atmosphere in which students who are interested in programming, Contests, Application, Development, Co-curricular activities and Projects can share their ideas, knowledge and proficiency with other like-minded students. The slogan of DUETCS is to learn, share & excel yourself... DUETCS also provides adequate facilities to members such as workshops, seminars and others. Through arrange the different workshops and competitions, students have learnt many different applications and software that they've not known or used before. DUETCS was arranged intra university programming contest, Project exhibition in several times. Lead by a Committee of dedicated professionals, academicians, research scientists and technology wizards, DUETCS prides itself in its contribution to the IT community in their strive to improve the quality of life and inclusive growth in the Society by the positive application of Information Communication Technology.
