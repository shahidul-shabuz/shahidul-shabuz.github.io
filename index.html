About Me

Teaching experience with a growing research profile

I serve as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Rajshahi Science and Technology University (RSTU), Natore, Bangladesh. Before this appointment, I served as a Lecturer at the same institution from October 2021 to April 2024.

I am currently pursuing an MSc Engineering degree in Computer Science and Engineering at Dhaka University of Engineering & Technology (DUET), Gazipur. I completed my first semester with a GPA of 4.00/4.00, and my second semester is in progress.

My research interests include explainable artificial intelligence, computer vision, deep learning, natural language processing, intelligent transportation systems, medical image analysis and graph neural networks. I aim to pursue doctoral research that combines methodological rigor with practical social impact.
