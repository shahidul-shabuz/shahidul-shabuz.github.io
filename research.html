Undergraduate Thesis

  • Brain Tumor MRI Image Segmentation Using Convolutional Neural Network (BSc Thesis, DUET, 2021)

    This undergraduate thesis focuses on automatic segmentation of brain tumors from MRI images using a CNN-based architecture, trained on LGG datasets with pre-processing, feature extraction and post-processing for accurate tumor region detection.

Research Proposal

  • Multi-Modal MRI-Based Brain Tumor Segmentation Using Integrated CNN, Transformer, and Attention Modules (Research Proposal, 2025)

    This research proposes a hybrid deep learning framework integrating CNN, Swin Transformer, spatial attention, channel attention, and cross-attention fusion to improve segmentation performance in multi-modal MRI scans (T1, T1c, T2, FLAIR), evaluated on BraTS and TCIA datasets.
